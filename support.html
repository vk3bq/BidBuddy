Legal & Estimate Notes

BidBuddy provides general information and estimates only for Australian property buying and auction bidding. It does not take into account your personal objectives, financial situation, or needs, and it is not financial, legal, tax, or lending advice.

All estimates are approximate. Please confirm duties, fees, concessions, grants, eligibility, and total costs with the relevant Australian authority or agency and a qualified adviser before you bid or buy.

If BidBuddy shows a potential first home buyer grant, you may be eligible depending on the state, property type, property value, transaction dates, and your circumstances. Confirm current rules and eligibility with the relevant authority before relying on the estimate.
