BidBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08/04/2026

What BidBuddy Stores

BidBuddy stores saved properties, notes, listing URLs, app settings, and any property photos you add locally on your device so you can manage calculations and saved properties.

What BidBuddy Does Not Do

BidBuddy does not use accounts and does not upload your saved properties, notes, or photos to a server managed by the app. BidBuddy also does not sell your personal information.

Photos and Camera

If you choose to add property photos, BidBuddy requests access to the camera or photo library only so you can attach those images to saved properties. You can revoke camera or photo access at any time in the iPhone Settings app.

Location and Address Search

When you use address lookup or open a property in Maps, BidBuddy uses Apple mapping services to return address suggestions and open locations in Apple Maps. Those requests are handled by Apple services when you choose to use those features.

No External Account or Sync

BidBuddy does not include user accounts, cloud sync, or a BidBuddy-hosted backend for your saved property data in this version of the app.

Diagnostics

BidBuddy registers for Apple MetricKit diagnostics and performance reports, such as crash, hang, launch, and responsiveness reports that Apple makes available to the app. In this version, those reports are stored locally on your device for troubleshooting and are not uploaded to a BidBuddy-managed server.

Retention and Deletion

Your saved properties, notes, photos, and locally stored diagnostics remain on your device until you delete them in BidBuddy or remove the app from your device. Deleting a saved property in BidBuddy also removes its saved photos from the device.

Pricing Rules Updates

BidBuddy can check its published pricing rules source for updates when the app starts. If that published update is unavailable, the app continues using bundled rules included with the app.
