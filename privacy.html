BidBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

BidBuddy is a personal property planning and bidding assistance app for Australian property buyers.

What BidBuddy Stores

BidBuddy stores saved properties, notes, listing URLs, app settings, and any property photos you choose to add locally on your device so you can manage calculations and saved properties.

What BidBuddy Does Not Do

BidBuddy does not require an account and does not upload your saved properties, notes, or photos to a server managed by the app. BidBuddy does not sell your personal information.

Camera and Photo Library

If you choose to add property photos, BidBuddy requests access to the camera or photo library only to let you attach those images to saved properties.

Address Lookup and Maps

When you use address lookup or open a property in Maps, BidBuddy uses Apple mapping services to return address suggestions and open locations in Apple Maps. Those requests are handled by Apple services when you choose to use those features.

Pricing Rules Updates

If a remote pricing rules XML URL is configured in the app, BidBuddy can download updated pricing rules from that source. If no remote URL is configured, the app uses bundled rules included with the app.

Important

This app provides estimates only and is not a formal financial, legal, tax, or lending tool. If BidBuddy later adds accounts, syncing, analytics, crash reporting, or cloud storage, this privacy policy and the App Store privacy details would need to change.

Contact

If you need to contact the developer about this privacy policy, use the support details provided on the app's App Store listing or repository.
